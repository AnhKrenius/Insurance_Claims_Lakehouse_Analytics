# KPI Definitions

## Claims Assigned in 3 Days
Percentage of claims assigned to a handler within 3 days of creation.

## Claims Closed in 30 Days
Percentage of claims closed within 30 days of creation.

## Average Claim Duration
Average number of days between claim creation and closure.

## Incident Severity Distribution
Claim counts grouped by severity level.

## Handler Efficiency
Average claim duration and volume per handler / manager.

## Platinum Customer Claims
Claims associated with customers in the Platinum segment.
